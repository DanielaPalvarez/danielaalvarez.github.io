Case: Strategy to Reduce Waste

This case outlines a timeline-based approach to implement a sustainable waste reduction strategy within an academic or organizational setting.

Week 1

Diagnosis & Audit

Analyze current waste generation across departments and identify the most critical sources.

Week 2

Awareness Campaign

Launch communication efforts, posters, talks, and infographics on campus to promote waste consciousness.

Week 3

Segregation System

Install and label bins for recyclables, organics, and general waste; train custodial staff accordingly.

Week 4

Pilot & Feedback

Implement the system in a pilot area. Collect data and feedback for improvement.

Week 5+

Full Implementation & Monitoring

Expand to the whole institution with weekly waste reports and incentives for top-performing areas.
